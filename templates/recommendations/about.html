About Green Travel

What is green travel?

Green travel means choosing transport options and behaviours that minimise greenhouse gas emissions and environmental impact.

Why it matters

Transport is a major contributor to CO₂ emissions. Choosing lower-carbon options helps reduce climate impact and preserve local environments.

How this system works

  1. Enter your source, destination and distance.
  2. The system estimates CO₂ emissions for common transport options.
  3. It recommends the lowest-emission option and shows estimated savings.
